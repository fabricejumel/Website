Fabrice Jumel is professor assistant  in [CPE Lyon](https://www.cpe.fr) (Engineering School). He is at the head of the robotic of service specialty . He is a member of the [CHROMA INRIA](https://team.inria.fr/chroma/en/)  team of the [CITI Lab](http://www.citi-lab.fr/)  (Cooperative and Human-aware Robot Navigation in Dynamic Environments). He like Robocup and Robocup like competitions. He is team leader of a robocup@home team and was previously team leader of robocup@work one.
 
He's more precisely involved in social navigation, fleet of robots, robot assistant for elder or disease peoples.
